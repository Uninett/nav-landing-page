Features

Alerts

If something in the network goes down, NAV alerts you via your dashboard, email or SMS.

Topology

Devices added to the NAV database can be visualized in several interactive network maps.

Statistics

NAV gives you VLAN graphs, link loads and customizable reports for your equipment.

Management

Use NAV to monitor your inventory and configure network equipment, such as switches.

About NAV

NAV (Network Administration Visualized) is a free software program to monitor networks, developed by Sikt - Norwegian Agency for Shared Services in Education and Research, and used by universities and corporations around the world.
