Monitor your network with ease.

Designed by Scandinavians, this free software makes network administration feel like flying.

Alerts

If something in the network goes down, NAV alerts you via your dashboard, and can even send you an SMS.

Topology

Devices added to the NAV database can be visualized in several interactive maps of your whole network.

Statistics

NAV gives you with VLAN graphs, link loads and customizable reports for your equipment.

Management

Use NAV to monitor your inventory and configure network equipment over SNMP, such as switch ports.

About NAV

NAV (Network Administration Visualized) is a free software program to monitor networks, developed by UNINETT and used by universities and corporations around the world.

Customizeable dashboard
Extensive statistical overviews
Configuration on the fly
Full traceability of users and equipment
Device and vendor agnostic
